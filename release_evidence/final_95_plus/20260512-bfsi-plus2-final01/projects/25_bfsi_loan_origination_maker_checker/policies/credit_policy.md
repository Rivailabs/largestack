# Credit Policy

## Eligibility Criteria
- KYC status must be verified.
- Credit score >= 700.
- Debt-to-income ratio <= 0.45.
- Requested amount <= annual income * 4.
- No days past due (dpd_days = 0).

## Approval
- Amounts >= 1,000,000 require maker-checker approval.
- Manual review for borderline cases.

# AML Policy

All transactions must be screened for sanctions. High-risk transactions require manual review.

## Screening Rules
- Blocked countries: Iran, North Korea, Syria, Cuba
- Amount spikes: >5x average monthly volume
- High-risk keywords: sanctions, terrorism, money laundering
- KYC score >= 80 is high risk

## SAR Filing
SARs are drafted internally and require approval before filing.

# IT Support

For a broken VPN: first reinstall the corporate VPN client from the self-service
portal, then restart. If it still fails, raise a support ticket — the IT team
responds to P1 (work-stopping) issues within 1 hour during business hours.

Laptop hardware failures are handled by IT; a loaner laptop can be provided for
urgent situations such as customer demos. Password resets are self-service via
the identity portal.

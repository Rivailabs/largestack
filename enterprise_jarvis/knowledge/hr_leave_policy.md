# HR Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue **24 annual leave days** per calendar year, plus 10
public holidays. Unused leave (up to 5 days) may be carried into the next year.

Sick leave is **12 days** per year and does not require manager pre-approval, but a
doctor's note is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

Parental leave is 26 weeks. Leave requests are submitted in the HR portal and
require manager approval for blocks longer than 5 working days.

# Largestack AI domain plan

## Primary domains

- Main website: https://largestack.ai
- Documentation: https://docs.largestack.ai
- SaaS app: https://app.largestack.ai
- API: https://api.largestack.ai

## Supporting domains

- Developer redirect: https://largestack.dev
- Protection redirect: https://largestack.tech

## Notes

`largestack.ai` is the primary company, SaaS, and public brand domain.

`largestack.dev` is for developer documentation, examples, and open-source discovery.

`largestack.tech` is a protection domain and should redirect to `https://largestack.ai`.

Subdomains like `app.largestack.ai`, `api.largestack.ai`, and `docs.largestack.ai` are DNS records under `largestack.ai`. They are not purchased separately.
